# Creator Voice Guardian

Use this skill after logic and evidence checks, before final polishing.

## Voice Principles

- Concrete beats grand.
- Field texture beats abstract summary.
- Leave room for uncertainty.
- Do not turn living people into concepts.
- Do not outsource the creator's judgment, taste, or relationship boundaries to AI.

## Workflow

1. Mark sentences that sound generic, promotional, or too complete.
2. Identify places where AI over-explains what the material should reveal.
3. Rewrite with:
   - concrete people
   - scenes
   - original phrasing
   - restraint
   - clear factual boundaries
4. Keep useful structure, but return the voice to the creator.

## Output

Use three sections:

- `不像我的地方`
- `为什么不稳`
- `更像我的改法`
