# Lite Workbench Templates

Use these templates when building the first version of a creator content workbench.

## 00_使用说明

Purpose:

- Tell the user where new material goes.
- Explain the intent tags.
- Explain which gates happen before publication.

Starter text:

```text
先把材料放进 01_素材收件箱，不急着写。
每条材料前加一个意图：入库 / 参考拆解 / 发布 / 产品化 / 验证 / 复盘。
每篇公开内容发布前都要经过事实与边界校对、去 AI 味检查。
```

## 01_素材收件箱

Fields:

- date
- source
- material type
- original link or file path
- intent tag
- privacy level
- next action

## 02_内容查看台

Questions:

- What is actually inside this material?
- Is there a person, scene, question, evidence, method, or product signal?
- Is it public, private, or unclear?
- Should it become a card, topic, draft, case, skill, or archive item?

## 03_素材卡

Card types:

- person card
- scene card
- quote card
- question card
- method card
- case card
- risk card
- product signal card

Each card should contain:

- source
- usable fragment
- why it matters
- public boundary
- possible outputs

## 04_选题池

Classify topics by:

- platform
- reader
- evidence strength
- energy cost
- public risk
- conversion or asset value

## 05_发布队列

Track:

- title or working title
- format
- platform
- current stage
- missing facts
- final gate status
- publish date
- recovery note

## 06_AI协作原则

Minimum rules:

- AI is the second workbench, not the author.
- Automate repetitive labor, not judgment.
- Evidence beats fluency.
- Keep human voice, relationship boundaries, and final responsibility visible.

## 07_去AI味检查清单

Check:

- over-neat contrast
- grand opening
- empty specificity
- no evidence but strong claim
- repeated structure
- generic ending
- loss of person, scene, relation, and voice

## 08_事实与边界校对清单

Check:

- time, place, names, titles, organizations
- source support
- privacy
- consent
- internal vs public information
- images and screenshots
- commercial or partner口径

## 09_发布后反馈回收

Recover:

- comments
- private messages
- saves
- shares
- referrals
- collaboration leads
- reader questions
- creator's own body sense after publishing

## 10_产品化回收

Ask:

- Is this a repeatable problem?
- Did someone else ask for it?
- Can it become a method, skill, template, workshop, consulting package, or offer?
- What proof exists?
